The Oberoi, DUBAI February 14 & 15, 2015

Welcome to the
World of Education !

WES 2015 is an Education Show taking a new approach in helping you tackle new challenges head on. We will bridge the gap between the students & Education Institutes. Students will have the opportunity to book appointments prior to visiting the show. This will enable YOU to be better prepared to answer the queries at the show.

A highly motivated generation of students are seeking the BEST educational courses available nationally or internationally in their study field of their choice. This trend has brought into focus an improved quality of enquiries of students who are better prepared with a relevant list of questions, enhancing the potential for conversion from enquiries to admission. World Education Show 2015 will showcase academic strengths of international colleges, universities & education institutes through national pavilions.

WES 2015 is timed keeping in mind the intake for the local & international universities for the academic year 2015-16. WES 2015 will offer education institutes and professional training providers the opportunity to drive up enrollment and reach out to the next batch of aspiring students.

It will help you engage your target audience, laying the groundwork for successful student conversions and increased marketing visibility.



EXHIBITORS, BE THERE TO:

  • Meet thousands of attendees including high school students and fresh graduates.
  • Discuss training and post-graduate courses with thousands of professionals interested in career development qualifications.
  • Network with key government entities and industry colleagues.
  • Save on marketing costs; take advantage of having your audience visit you directly.
